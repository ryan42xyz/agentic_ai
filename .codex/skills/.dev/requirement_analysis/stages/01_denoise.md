# Stage 01 â€” Denoise (Requirement De-noising)

## Objective

Understand **what is explicitly stated** in the requirement,
without interpreting intent or proposing solutions.

This stage answers:
- What is actually said?
- What is explicitly requested?
- What information is missing but required?

## Instructions

From the input (product message, issue, PR, or description):

### 1. Explicit Facts
List only facts that are directly stated.
No interpretation, no inference.

### 2. Explicit Goals
Rewrite the stated goals verbatim or near-verbatim.

### 3. Missing Critical Information
List information that is REQUIRED to proceed but NOT provided.

Examples:
- success criteria
- scope boundaries
- constraints
- users / callers
- environment assumptions

## Forbidden

- No guessing intent
- No proposing design
- No "usually" or "likely" statements
