# Stage 03: Draft

## Purpose

Write a clear article draft strictly following the locked outline. Act as a technical editor, not an author.

## Inputs

- Locked outline from Stage 02
- Extracted structure from Stage 01

## Procedure

1. Follow the locked outline exactly
2. Write clear, coherent prose for each section
3. Use the beliefs and content from the extracted structure
4. Do NOT add new conclusions or ideas
5. You may clarify language, but not meaning
6. If something is missing, insert TODO instead of inventing content

## Outputs

This stage produces:
- A complete draft article in Markdown format
- The draft follows the locked outline structure
- Content is based on the extracted beliefs and structure
- TODOs mark any missing content that needs author input

Output format: Markdown only.

## Constraints

- Follow the outline exactly
- Do NOT add new conclusions or ideas
- You may clarify language, but not meaning
- If something is missing, insert TODO instead of inventing content
- Act as a technical editor, not an author

## Stop Conditions

Stop and wait for human approval if:
- The locked outline is unclear or incomplete
- After completing the draft, always wait for human approval before proceeding
