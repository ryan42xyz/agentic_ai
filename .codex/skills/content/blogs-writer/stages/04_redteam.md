# Stage 04: Red Team

## Purpose

Critically attack the draft to identify weaknesses, missing boundaries, counterexamples, and failure modes. Act as a skeptical reviewer.

## Inputs

- Draft article from Stage 03
- Locked outline from Stage 02 (for reference)

## Procedure

1. Read the draft carefully
2. Identify weak or underspecified claims
3. Find missing boundaries and assumptions
4. Consider counterexamples
5. Identify real-world failure modes
6. Determine what evidence would validate the claims
7. Do NOT rewrite the article
8. Do NOT soften critique

## Outputs

This stage produces:
- A critical review in Markdown format with clear sections covering:
  - Weak or underspecified claims
  - Missing boundaries
  - Counterexamples
  - Real-world failure modes
  - Evidence needed to validate claims

Output format: Markdown with clear sections.

## Constraints

- Do NOT rewrite the article
- Do NOT soften critique
- Be thorough and critical
- Focus on identifying problems, not solving them

## Stop Conditions

Stop and wait for human approval if:
- The draft has fundamental issues that need author attention
- After completing the red-team review, always wait for human approval before proceeding
